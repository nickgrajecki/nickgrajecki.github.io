About



I am a graduate of BA (Hons) VFX from the Norwich University of Arts. I have had a passion for motion graphics and VFX since I was very young - for years my main hobby was creating cinematic shorts using video game footage clips. I taught myself Adobe After Effects and that kick-started my passion for all forms of visual design and storytelling. During my education, I gradually shifted my main focus from VFX to motion graphics work - as reflected in my showreel and examples of work. I have worked with many eSports and gaming organisations in the past - including companies such as Multiplay and Elevate.gg. My roles varied, but I worked as a video editor, motion graphics artist and general production assistant. I have also been commissioned to do freelance work for a variety of social media influencers. If you're looking for a freelancer to do some motion graphic work for you or your company, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Insomnia Gaming Festival

Motion Graphics Designer for live show content


In 2016 I volunteered for the Multiplay Insomnia Gaming Festival 58 in Birmingham. I was placed with the video production company employed for the festival, working on various pieces of content required for the various events taking place at the NEC. The highlight was working on the motion graphics for the Drone Racing Series.

Top5 Central

Video editor and motion designer


I was approached and asked to produce some commission videos for Top5 Central - a popular YouTube channel (3.4 million subscribers) creating viral ranking videos. My work was very well-received and I was eventually offered a full time position as the head editor - however, due to other obligations at the time, I was forced to pass on the opportunity. My video, at the time of writing, had achieved over to 220,000 views.
